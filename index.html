An Interactive Social Community For All Muslim People

We are a social community of muslim peoples. We come together to educate each other on issues related to our religion and take part in vital discussions to help strengthen our deen. We also donate monthly to orphanages and contribute to the development of mosques and our community in general.

Our Story

Muslimah was created to enable the connection of muslims all over the world, to make them know they are not alone and each and Everyone of us are together! To answer their questions about religion, to strengthen their faith and to contribute and give to the society, donate to orphanages, build more mosques and madrashahs and in General, improve the muslim society and the world at large!

How We Meet!

We have servers where we all discuss issues that are meant to talk about. We host video conference calls every once in a while, and broadcast live on our Youtube channel twice every month, inviting scholars to lecture us on chosen topics. We sometimes also meet physically on agreed location and the event will also be broadcasted live for the people that cannot attend.
